Our Institutional Manifesto

A philosophical charter transformed into a genuine global action plan.

CFI does not seek to divide. We seek to gather. We do not seek to impose. We seek to inspire. We do not seek to destroy. We seek to build. We do not seek to oppose peoples. We seek to build bridges between them. OUR CALL TO THE WORLD: Regardless of your nation. Regardless of your religion. Regardless of your culture. Regardless of your profession. If you believe in human dignity, in education, in peace, in integrity and in the future of future generations, then you can contribute to building a better world with us. This is extraordinary. This text on the 10 Historical Contributions and the Call to the World shifts the CFI from a philosophical charter to a true global action plan.

Vision & Core Values

The guiding ethical pillars of our actions across the globe.

Awakening of Consciences

Fostering critical thinking, constructive dialogue, perspective, and mindful action from every citizen for the common good.

Unity of Peoples

Transcending cultural, national, and religious divisions to build a solid fraternity rooted in mutual respect and active cooperation.

Human Dignity

Ensuring and promoting the inalienable respect of every individual, regardless of their social background or profession.

The 10 Historical Contributions of the CFI

Our doctrinal and strategic roadmap for future generations.

01

Awakening of Consciences

Helping citizens think, analyze, and engage in clear, meaningful dialogue.

02

Global Education

Creating books, guides, educational posters, and free training sessions.

03

Culture & Dialogue

Encouraging mediation, mutual respect, and global social cohesion.

04

Human Dignity

Promoting unconditional respect for every person, regardless of their origins.

05

Dignity of Work

Fostering the understanding that every honest profession actively builds our shared world.

06

Youth Protection

Providing young generations with solid reference pillars, practical skills, and real opportunities.

07

Equitable Cooperation

Promoting win-win partnerships and the fair sharing of knowledge worldwide.

08

Cultural Preservation

09

Sustainable Innovation

Encouraging solutions that enhance education, healthcare, and the planetary environment.

10

Future Generations

Ensuring that every modern project leaves a healthy, positive, and viable legacy for tomorrow's children.
